Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

1. Overview

Universal Football operates this website and a companion application used to measure the performance of our own social media content. We collect as little data as possible.

2. What we collect

  • Website visitors: we do not set cookies, run analytics scripts, or collect personal information on this site.
  • Our application: connects via platform APIs (such as the TikTok Display API and YouTube Data API) to read metrics — views, likes, comments, shares — for content published by our own accounts only. It does not access, collect or store data about any other user.

3. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties.
  • We do not track visitors across other websites.
  • We do not collect data from viewers of our clips.

4. Data storage

API access tokens and retrieved metrics are stored locally on our own equipment and are used exclusively for internal performance analysis. Access tokens can be revoked at any time from the connected platform's security settings.

5. Third-party platforms

Our content is published on YouTube and TikTok, which have their own privacy policies governing your interactions with them.
